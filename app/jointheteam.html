Join the MedProof Team

We're building a friendly, reliable team of UK medical students and recent graduates who want to help the next generation of medics succeed. Whether you're a confident writer, an experienced tutor, or simply enthusiastic about supporting applicants — we'd love to hear from you.

We’re especially looking for:

  • Current UK medical students (any year)
  • Recent graduates or FY doctors
  • People with mentoring or tutoring experience

You’ll help review structured UCAS personal statements, provide meaningful feedback, and support applicants throughout the process. All contributions are paid, flexible, and remote.

To apply: Email info.medproof@gmail.com with a short paragraph about who you are, your year of training, and why you’re interested in joining.
