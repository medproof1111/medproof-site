Who We Are

MedProof was founded by UK medical students who understand the stress and pressure of applying to medicine. We know how it feels to want honest, actionable feedback — and that’s what we deliver.

“We don’t just give you edits — we help you think like an admissions tutor.”

We’re here to level the playing field. No matter your background or school, you deserve support from people who’ve done it successfully — and care about your future.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike generic editing platforms or AI tools, MedProof gives you personalised, human feedback crafted by current or recent medical students. Every comment you receive is thoughtful, strategic, and focused on helping you meet UCAS criteria and stand out.

And with a 72-hour turnaround, you won’t be left waiting.

How It Works

Step 1: Choose a package and pay securely.

Step 2: Receive your unique MedProof ID by email within 24 hours.

Step 3: Email your structured personal statement (PDF or Word) to info.medproof@gmail.com with your ID in the subject.

Step 4: Receive detailed feedback within 72 hours.

Step 5: On a 3-round package? Send two more drafts with your ID for further review.
