UCAS Personal Statement Guide Library

These guides are written by current and recent UK medical students to help you tackle the new structured UCAS personal statement questions. Whether you're starting from scratch or refining a final draft, our goal is to give you clear, actionable advice — the kind we wish we'd had.

Each guide breaks down what the question is really asking, how to structure your response, and how to avoid common pitfalls. We want you to feel confident in your writing and empowered in your application.
